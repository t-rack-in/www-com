About the solution

The project consists of delivering a complete asset tracking solution, mainly with a focus on reverse logistics.

The solution uses technologies such as RFID to receive traces of the detected assets, however, it is not limited to this technology, it is enough to receive only the minimum information to record the history of the specific asset.

We know that it is not enough to just know where a particular asset is positioned geographically in real time. Nowadays this type of approach is the starting point for a solution with such scope.

Internal rules for audits, time monitoring of those responsible, and the involvement of supervisors and subordinates are even more important for a satisfactory result.

With a focus on sustainability, the solution monitors the useful life of assets and ensures automated alert for supervisors and subordinates to know exactly what should be discarded while strictly respecting their useful life.

Knowing exactly which assets to monitor, which assets have questionable behavior, what these idle assets can influence in the day-to-day life of the organization: this is possible with T-RACK.IN

Features

Multi Companies

Manage all you asset tracking of all companies in only one system with our quick access feature and shared data reports.

Lifetime Control

In the system, you can control the date of disposal of your assets and to be alerted when the useful life is reached.

Audit System

Take care of your assets through audits with responsible, assets to be analyzed and traceability throughout the analysis process.

Custom Alerts

With t-rack.in you configure alerts (Call, SMS, Webhook and E-mail) to monitor undue detections in the movement of assets.

Management Reports

At the system, you will have a high number of reports for collecting asset management information for supervisors.

Done for you

Extra data automatically available according to the category and control of specific activities. Example: Bags, Cattle and others.

Scheduled Inventory

Create counting routines programmed through the system and obtain the result of your inventory daily.

Integrations

Your development team will be able to integrate your system (ERP, PCP, etc.) easily through our API and Webhooks.

About Company

T-RACK.IN is a Brazilian Startup focused on technological tools for reverse logistics in commercial, industrial and agribusiness areas.

Beginning in 2019, the company entered into partnerships with Chinese equipment manufacturers so that it could start testing and approving possible equipment on the market.

In the first quarter of 2020, the company presented the main solution and also the strategy to develop its own equipment to reduce dependence on foreign markets.
